Interior

Automotive Interiors - Upholstery Restoration or Customization Restore your vehicle’s classic interior to original condition and revive its unique character. Or design a brand new, custom theme that will stand out from the rest. Whatever your design style, maximize function and create an enjoyable driving environment with a well planned and meticulously executed interior. All services designed to turn your project into a Unique Ride and masterful show piece. We have over 80 years of combined experience ready to work on your project.

Original Interior Restoration

We have multiple sources that provide us with original material in order to stitch up your restored interior back to the way it looked when it was new. In addition we provide install services if you choose to select an aftermarket original style kit interior.

Custom Hand Crafted Interiors

If you want a one off custom interior that is completely hand crafted and is sure to be a real show winner then let us show you what we can do. Our interiors have won numerous national shows and have appeared in many magazines. Each hand crafted interior is unique to each project and that design will only be used in your vehicle.

Headliner Replacement

We are experts in the art of installing headliners, no wrinkles and the fit is like new. We can sew the replacement headliner up ourselves or install an original style replacement headliner, Cloth or Vinyl the results will look original.

Carpet Replacement

Is your carpet sun faded or just old & damaged? We can replace your carpet with original style material; we have sources for all makes & models. We provide sound/heat shield services as well if you would like to upgrade your vehicle while replacing the carpet.
