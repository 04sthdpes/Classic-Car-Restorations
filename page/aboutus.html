About Us

At the helm of every Classic Restorations of Nasika project is founder and president Dipesh Shrestha. A lifelong auto enthusiast and collector, an avid sports fan and an ex professional Nepalese football player, Art turned his passions into an internationally recognized specialty auto restoration and modification business.

Art has over 5 years experience in restoration, resto modding, street rodding, fabrication and custom painting and personally oversees and directs the extensive crew at Classic Restorations of Nasika.

Art is actively involved in every project that comes through the garage, from installing exhaust systems to reshaping body panels, and performs extensive quality checks at the end of a restoration. He is supported by a staff of highly skilled mechanics and artisans who work on ten to fifteen projects at a time.

When Art is not at the shop he can be found on the ice!
